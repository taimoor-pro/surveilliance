ICSC Las Vegas 2023

The retail security experts from Alasar Survellience Monitoring are excited for ICSC Las Vegas, the shopping center industry’s premier event. As the North American leader in live video monitoring solutions, we look forward to showcasing our cutting-edge technology to the industry’s finest.

Our proactive security solution is customized for the unique challenges of retail and shopping centers. By leveraging the power of advanced video analytics combined with human intelligence, we can help safeguard valuable assets and deter losses from break-ins, vandalism, damage and other unwanted activities.

Alasar Survellience’s retail security specialists will be at Booth 852X – South Hall Lower at the Las Vegas Convention Center to share videos, case studies and other information about how our live video monitoring has helped shopping centers across North America reduce loss, increase safety and enhance their overall operations. You can schedule a time to meet with them at the show to discuss your business needs and how you can achieve better security coverage for less than the cost of traditional security solutions.

We look forward to seeing you there!
