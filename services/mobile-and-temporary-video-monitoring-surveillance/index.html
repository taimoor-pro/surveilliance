Mobile Video Monitoring

Surveillance For Temporary or Remote Sites

Based on years of experience securing construction sites, Stealth’s Igloo Surveillance Solution has been designed to exceed the features required to secure even the most challenging sites. Built for rapid deployment, the Igloo solution can be mounted on almost any pole or flat surface, like the roof of a site management trailer.

Construction sites present one of the most difficult environments to secure, with unstable power, poor lighting and constant change. Stealth’s Igloo Surveillance Solution was built specifically to overcome these challenges and has evolved over the past 15+ years into a leading premier security solution. Key features include:

  • Self-contained surveillance solution
  • Battery backup for dealing with site power outages
  • Quick deployment
  • Installation on a pole or roof
  • Expandable to support additional cameras
  • Operates with wireless or hardwired internet
  • Supports cameras along with live talk-down technology
  • Can be placed in many locations
  • View cameras from PCs, laptops or mobile devices
