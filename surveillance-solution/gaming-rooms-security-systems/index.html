Gaming rooms Security Systems

Our unified security agents keep track of gaming tables with video analytics and live streaming of video to identify theft or cheating in real time. We Keep track of everything that’s happening at the gaming tables with live, streaming video. Rewind, fast-forward, or zoom in for a closer look. Pinpoint the location of alarms at access control points. Identify suspicious transactions at point of sale and cash cages on a visual, interactive map-based interface.

The largest users of surveillance systems in a single location can be found on the grounds of casinos. Casinos use integrated surveillance security to protect their customers and their business. A single casino can have camera counts that can total in the thousands. It is often noticeable that casinos have security cameras everywhere. Casinos face many regulations regarding the use of surveillance footage and instances of lost footage.
